We do slow coffee, on purpose.

Dis cyd started with a small roaster in a garage in Mafikeng. George wanted a place that felt like a deep breath. No rush. No laptops shouting. Just good music, honest food, and coffee that makes you pause.

We source directly from farmers in Yirgacheffe and Nyeri. Roasted in small batches every Monday. Served with intention.
